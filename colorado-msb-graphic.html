The Colorado Anomaly: How a Crypto Exemption Broke the MSB Map

FinCEN self-reported registration data reveals an unprecedented clustering of money services businesses in Colorado following the state's 2018 regulatory carve-out.

Registrant Profile Anomalies

Colorado's exemption of cryptocurrency businesses from state money transmitter licensing, combined with FinCEN's self-reported registration framework, created a low-friction pathway. Entities exploited this at unprecedented rates, registering shell-like operations with maximum jurisdictional claims and minimal physical presence.
